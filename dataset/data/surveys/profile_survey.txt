Q2. How would you describe the vibes of your outfits and wardrobe 
(select all that apply). If an option does not make sense to you, 
please ignore it.
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Options included (multi-select):
- Chic – Elegant, polished, often minimalist  
- Preppy – Clean-cut, collegiate, often includes polos, blazers  
- Streetwear – Trend-driven, urban, sneakers, oversized fits  
- Casual – Everyday wear, laid-back, basic staples  
- Sporty / Athleisure – Athletic-inspired, leggings, sneakers  
- Minimalist – Simple cuts, neutral tones, low ornamentation  
- Vintage – Retro aesthetics, often 70s–90s inspired  
- Grunge – Dark tones, distressed fabrics, layered  
- Classic – Timeless cuts and colors, traditional  
- Goth – Dark, dramatic, black-heavy ensembles  
- Y2K – 2000s nostalgia, playful, low-rise, shiny materials  
- E-girl / E-boy – Online-influenced, alt fashion, anime  
- High Concept – Fashion-forward, unconventional, runway-inspired pieces  
- Business Casual – Office-appropriate, structured but not formal  
- Luxury – Designer-driven, high-end textures and brands  
- Cozy / Loungewear – Comfortable, soft fabrics, homey feel  
- Techwear – Futuristic, utilitarian, function-oriented
