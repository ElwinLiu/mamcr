



### Updated Taste Profile: User S1

**Overall Style Philosophy:**  
User S1 adopts a **"practical and polished casual"** approach, seeking a balance between comfort, high functionality, and a subtle, curated aesthetic that avoids looking high-effort or flashy.

---

#### 1. Core Priorities
*   **Highest Priority:** Comfort (5/5) and Color (5/5).
*   **Secondary Focus:** Practicality (4/5) and Sustainability (4/5).
*   **Low Priority:** Indifferent to current trends, brand names, and price (1/5).

#### 2. Aesthetic & Color Preferences
*   **Favored Tones:** Deep greens, grays, and darker, grounded neutral palettes.
*   **Avoidances:** Bright colors (specifically white) and loud "statement" pieces. They prefer to blend in socially rather than stand out.
*   **Vibe:** Elevated casual, effortless, and energetic.

#### 3. Material & Technical Standards
*   **Natural Fibers:** A conscious preference for natural materials (e.g., wool) over synthetics; however, they will prioritize **technical performance (waterproofing, windproofing)** even if it requires synthetic materials in high-exposure outdoor contexts.
*   **Informed & Methodical Selection:** Decision-making is heavily driven by material properties and technical specifications.
*   **Technical Performance:** Specifically requires items to be **waterproof/weatherproof** and highly **breathable** to maintain temperature regulation and comfort during activity.
*   **Easy Maintenance:** Looks for materials that are dirt-repellent or easy to clean to handle exposure to outdoor debris.

#### 4. Versatility & Context
*   **Multi-Contextual:** Favors "basic staples" but will accept **performance-oriented outdoor gear (e.g., hiking-specific jackets)** for casual social outings (like farm visits) if the functional requirements of the setting necessitate it.
*   **Sustained Performance:** Requires gear capable of maintaining comfort and functionality during continuous use for **several hours** at a time.
*   **Outdoor Durability:** Items must handle specific terrains like grassy fields and dirt paths while protecting against cool temperatures and precipitation.

#### 5. Shopping Habit
*   **Frequency/Budget:** Typically shops yearly with a monthly budget allocation of $50 - $100.
